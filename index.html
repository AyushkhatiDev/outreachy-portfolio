About This Portfolio

This portfolio showcases my contributions to the Fedora Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) initiative during the Outreachy application phase. My work focused on documentation improvements, inclusive onboarding, and DEI strategy enhancement.

Through this experience, I've gained valuable insights into open-source collaboration, technical documentation, and community engagement strategies that foster inclusivity in technology spaces.

Learn About Fedora DEI

Outreachy Program Overview

Outreachy provides internships in open source and open science for people subject to systemic bias and impacted by underrepresentation in the technical industry.

  • Paid, remote internships
  • Focuses on increasing diversity in open source
  • Offers mentorship and real-world project experience
  • Empowers participants to become long-term contributors
